Frequently Asked Questions

What map formats does GeoPDF Viewer support?

GeoPDF Viewer supports ArcGIS-compatible GeoPDF files, including USGS topo maps and ArcGIS exports. The PDF must contain georeferencing data (GPTS/LPTS) to show your GPS location on the map.

How do I import a map?

Tap the "+" button on the map list screen to import a GeoPDF from your Files app or iCloud Drive.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → GeoPDF Viewer → Cancel Subscription.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open GeoPDF Viewer, tap "Restore Purchases" on the subscription screen, and sign in with the same Apple ID used to subscribe.
